Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 05/05/2025

Overview

At Gabriel Studio, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle, store, and protect any personal data you may provide while using our platform.

By accessing or using Gabriel Studio, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Gabriel Studio currently does not require user registration and does not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or contact details.

However, the platform may collect limited technical data to improve functionality and performance

Device & Usage Information

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Screen resolution
  • Pages visited and interaction patterns

Local Storage

We may use local storage or session storage to remember user preferences like theme mode, playback settings, or recently played tracks. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted or shared.

How We Use Your Information

We use the limited data we collect to

  • Improve the functionality and user experience of the platform
  • Maintain responsive layouts and player behavior across devices
  • Debug performance issues or interface inconsistencies
  • Analyze general, non-identifiable usage patterns (if analytics are added)

We do not use this information for advertising or user profiling.

Cookies and Tracking

Gabriel Studio may use cookies strictly for user session handling and playback settings.

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising scripts.

For more information, please refer to our Cookies Policy

Third-Party Services

At present, Gabriel Studio does Not integrate any third-party services that collect personal data.

In future updates, optional integrations like Firebase, user authentication, or analytics tools may be introduced. In such cases, this policy will be updated accordingly and users will be notified.

Data Security

Although we do not store or transmit personal data at this stage, we are committed to maintaining a secure codebase and following best practices in web development to ensure user privacy.

Children's Privacy

Gabriel Studio is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or store information from children.

Your Rights

Since Gabriel Studio does not collect identifiable personal information, there is no need for data access, correction, or deletion requests. If personal features (e.g., login or user profiles) are added in the future, users will be informed of their rights and data control options.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect platform changes, improvements, or new features. All changes will be published on this page with the latest effective date.
